Tugeraneyo

TUGERANEYO is a platform linking Passengers and Private Drivers going in the same direction, for both to share same vehicle and related Transport Costs equitably. The purpose of the platform is to provide more flexible and less expensive transport opportunities to passengers and to reduce transport cost for vehicle owners.
